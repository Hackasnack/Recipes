Biscuits and Gravy

1 pound breakfast sausage, hot, sage or regular 3 cups milk pinch of poultry seasoning (optional) 3 tbls of regular flour 2 tbls butter salt and pepper to taste (needs quite a bit of salt) How to make sausage gravy! Its easy and really good! If you have some leftover gravy just put it in a pan and warm up with some more milk in it to get it thinned out again and its just as good as the first time.
